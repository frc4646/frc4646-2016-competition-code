	Left: M9

	Port: A0
Left PID Counter:
	Port: D4

	Port: D2
Left Launch Wheel Controller:
	Port: M8
Right Launch Wheel Controller:
	Port: M2

	Port: M7
Intake Arms:
	Motor Port: M5

	Left Encoder (A): D1
	Left Encoder (B): D0
	Right Encoder (A): D7
	Right Encoder (B): D8

	Port: M4
Write Inputs:
	Input Port: A2
